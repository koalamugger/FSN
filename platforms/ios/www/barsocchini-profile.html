Professional Experience

    Tammie serves as Chief Financial Officer and Vice President of the Business Transitions Team within The Financial Services Network. As Chief Financial Officer, Tammie is responsible for all corporate financial administration of The Network and handles all human resources for the office. As Vice President of the Business Development, Transitions and Integrations Team, Tammie focuses the efforts of the team on ensuring a smooth transition of advisors and their staff into The Network and LPL Financial by assisting in all aspects of the transition process.
    Tammie joined The Network in 1991 and has assumed a number of different roles and responsibilities during her tenure. With a high level of experience and expertise in the industry, Tammie and her team take a hands-on approach to helping advisors stay focused on what they need to know before and during the transition, allowing them to maximize their time with clients.
    Tammie lives in the East Bay area with her husband. She enjoys spending her free time with her family and hiking with their chocolate Labrador, Kona.
